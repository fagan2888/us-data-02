Maps and GIS Data

GIS Data - Download GIS data files. Each dataset consists of a shapefile(s) and supporting metadata.

  • Atlantic OCS Wind Planning Areas and Wind Energy Areas Shapefile – The current status of areas being considered for wind power development
  • Atlantic OCS Wind Planning Areas and Wind Energy Areas Metadata
  • Renewable Energy Leasing Areas Shapefile – Commercial wind energy leasing area boundaries
  • Renewable Energy Leasing Areas Metadata
  • Mid-Atlantic Wildlife Surveys – Study areas and tracklines of recent (since 2005) wildlife surveys conducted on the OCS
  • AIS - BOEM Vessel Traffic Density Analysis 2010 - Total number of vessels transiting each leasing aliquot during 2010 for the Atlantic OCS and coastal waters
  • Anglers’ Guide to the United States Atlantic Coast – Digital conversion of anglers’ atlases published by the National Marine Fisheries Service between 1974-1976
    • Geodatabase
    • Georeferenced Maps
  • New Jersey’s Recreational and Commercial Ocean Fishing Grounds – Digital conversion of a fishing grounds atlas published by the New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection in 1982
    • Geodatabase
    • Georeferenced Maps
