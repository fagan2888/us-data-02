Identification_Information: Citation: Citation_Information: Originator: NOAA/NOS/ORCA/SEA Publication_Date: 1994 Title: Medium Resolution Digital Vector U.S. Shoreline [OL 72 18.7 -66.5 -168] Publication_Information: Publication_Place: Silver Spring, MD Publisher: NOAA/NOS/ORCA/SEA Online_Linkeage: ftp://seaserver.nos.noaa.gov/datasets/shoreline Description: Abstract: NOAA's Medium Resolution Digital Vector U.S. Shoreline is a high-quality, Geographic Information System-ready, general-use digital vector data set created by the Strategic Environmental Assessments Division of NOAA's Office of Ocean Resources Conservation and Assessment. Compiled from more than 270 NOAA coast charts, it comprises over 75,000 nautical miles of coastline (nearly 2.5 million vertices), representing the entire coterminous United States of America. Alaska, the Hawaiian Islands, Puerto Rico, and all other interests and territories of the United States are not included in the collection. The product is designed for use by a broad audience, including government agencies, universities, research institutions, and individuals in the private sector.
