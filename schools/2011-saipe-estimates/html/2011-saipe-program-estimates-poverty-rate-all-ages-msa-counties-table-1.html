Title
SAIPE Highlights, 2011
Content
The following pages list counties with the highest and lowest 2011 SAIPE estimates of poverty rate and median household income.
Estimates for specific counties shown may not be statistically different from estimates for other counties shown or not shown, even if there is no overlap in their confidence intervals.
